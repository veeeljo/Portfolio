Biography

My name is Veljko Vilotijevic, I was born on August 29, 1998. in Belgrade. I finished elementary school "Stevan Sindjelic" in 2013 in Belgrade. As an early teenager, I became interested in design and multimedia and I am enrolling in the high school of electrical engineering “Nikola Tesla ”for the educational profile of multimedia electrician in 2013 in Belgrade. After 4 years my interest increased, and in 2017 I enrolled in the “College of Vocational Studies for IT ”for the computer multimedia program, where I continue my studies for design. Current desires and plans are to enter the professional world design, work abroad, gathering as much knowledge as possible and exploiting it every opportunity that arises. At the moment, I see myself as a graphic designer eager for work, knowledge and gaining new contacts.
